Video Games and their shift towards the MetaVerse

Video games have been a significant part for players in these recent years, and they're constantly expanding into a unified hub of experiences known as a Metaverse.

By definition, a Metaverse is a virtual reality space in which users can interact with other users in a computer generated space. In popular media, they have been depicted in a futuristic way of interacting with other people across the world. However, as technology seems to be advancing, this reality is closer than ever.


Games that have been changing their focus towards the metaverse typically include massive online multiplayer games on which they allow users to freely create and share experiences they themselves have created within said video game.
