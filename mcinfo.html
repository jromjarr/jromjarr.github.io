Released in a beta state in 2009, Minecraft is a sandbox game which is currently the world's best selling game. In it, users explore a procedurrally generated world, which varies completely from every world the user makes. It features 2 different modes; survival, which throws the player into the world withno tools, making them explore and use the world around them to shape their own personalized experience: and creative, which gives the player every single type of item found in the game, allowing them to freely use their creativity and make whatever is in their heart's desire.

A screenshot of a player-built house in the alpha version.

Since 2018, the game has been focusing on uniting players together, since its "Better Together" update allowed players to play together even if they were on different platforms for the first time, including Xbox, mobile, PC, and later on PlayStation players. This was the first time a video game allowed players to be in a multiplayer game, disregarding their platform of choice.


Other than uniting players together, it has also helped teach computer science, chemistry and different subjects in educational environments. It averages on around 1M players daily, making it one of the most played online games in a monthly span, with an average of 150M monthly players. Its different personalization options and ease of access to play with friends make it remain relevant after a decade since its launch.

A chemistry set in Minecraft: Education Edition. A video throughly explaining on how to teach students using Minecraft.

The game allows users to upload their own custom skins, builds and worlds, via their own marketplace. In here, other users can purchase these cosmetics and personalize their experience even further.
